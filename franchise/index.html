The Item 19 Difference

Item 19 Is Not Binary.

Most evaluation tools treat Item 19 as present or absent. Layer8 uses a 5-level disclosure quality taxonomy — because a fully audited FULL disclosure with quartile data is not the same as a revenue-only disclosure with 28 units.

★★★★★
FULL — Audited, Quartile Data

Revenue, expenses, and net income with quartile breakdown. Independently audited. Best possible disclosure.

★★★★☆
FULL — Revenue & Profit

Complete revenue and profit data. Not quartile-broken or audited. Still strong.

★★★☆☆
REVENUE_ONLY / PARTIAL

Gross sales only, or partial expense disclosure. Supplement with franchisee interviews.

★☆☆☆☆
NONE

No Item 19 provided. Franchisor has not disclosed financial performance. Mandatory franchisee interviews required.
