About Layer8 Tech Group

Built by Practitioners.

Layer8 applies enterprise technology and AI expertise to the business decisions that matter most — selling a company, buying one, or investing in a franchise.

The Story

Enterprise Expertise, Applied to SMB.

Layer8 Tech Group was founded in August 2025 in Marietta, Georgia, by enterprise technology leaders with 35+ years of collective experience at organizations including CVS Health, Aetna, and Encompass Digital Media.

The founding observation: the same structured assessment frameworks that enterprise buyers use in M&A diligence — risk scoring, domain-weighted evaluation, multi-factor scoring models — were not available to the SMB market in an accessible, affordable form.

Layer8 was built to change that. Three AI-powered assessment products in the first year. A fourth in development. All built on the same principle: structured, scored, defensible analysis — not subjective opinion.

Built on the Layer8 AI Chassis

Every Layer8 product is built on a shared infrastructure layer — the Layer8 AI Chassis — that enforces safety filtering, audit logging, PII detection, and the adapter pattern that prevents vendor lock-in. The chassis is open-source and publicly available on GitHub.

AI Governance

Responsible AI — By Design, Not by Policy.

Layer8 assessment products are designed to the NIST AI Risk Management Framework from the ground up — not retrofitted after the fact.

Our AI governance policy, permitted model list, prohibited use categories, and compliance roadmap are publicly documented and version-controlled on GitHub. Every assessment report carries a generated-by disclosure and a model version stamp.

NIST AI RMF Aligned

Govern, Map, Measure, Manage — all four functions of the NIST AI Risk Management Framework addressed in our governance documentation.

Safety Filtering on All Outputs

Every assessment output passes through safety and PII filters before delivery. No raw model output reaches a client report.

Human-in-the-Loop

All consequential assessment scores are reviewed by a Layer8 analyst before report delivery. AI accelerates — humans sign off.

Audit Logging

Every model inference call is logged with model version, timestamp, input hash, and output hash. Full audit trail for every assessment delivered.
